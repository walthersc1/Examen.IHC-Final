The 10 Best Cafés of Antalya

Like we mentioned in our Istanbul review, Turkey (read more about their coffee culture here) is the place where you can get some of the best coffee in the world. This southern city in Turkey is a great summer tourist place. With its Azure blue sea, golden beaches and world famous Turkish hospitality make this city a tourist paradise. Whatever you are doing while here, and there is a lot to do (driving quads, jeep safari, aqua parks), you will need to regain all of that spent energy. There are many ancient landmarks like Termessos or Kalcici, that takes time and energy to explore. Aqua parks are so much fun, but will make you feel exhausted afterwards. Check out the must see landmarks of Antalya by visiting this site! If you prefer being on the beaches, you will want to cool off with one ice coffee in a nearby café. Whichever activity you choose to do, most important thing is to have fun while doing it. Since there is so much to do here, in this article, we will help you find some excellent cafés for you to choose which suits you best. Then just sit down, have a good time and enjoy the coffee.

10. Lal Cafe

  • Address: Iskele Caddesi | No:2, Antalya, Turkey
  • Telephone: +90 242 244 6236
  • Opening hours: Mon – Fri, 07:00 – 00:00; Sat – Sun, 07:00 – 01:00
  • More information about the coffee house: n/a

Lal Cafe is small, but welcoming place to visit. It is done as a small hut where the coffee is made, with a lot of outdoor sitting room. The whole place is surrounded by flora which is making a lot of shade (well needed on hot summer days). Over the café are many flags from different countries, to show that this café is international. Staff is nice and chill like the café. There is no table service, so you have to order from the bar, but they will always greet you with a smile and a helping hand. Since this is Turkey we are talking about, you can expect to find traditional Turkish coffee on the menu. You can also find regular types of coffee, like espresso.

They offer more than one menu, depending on the time of day. All of them are filled with many delicious things to drink and eat. Beside coffee, you can try Raki (traditional Turkish alcoholic drink) that is serve in a glass, which is inside a metal container filled with frozen water. It will keep the temperature of the drink refreshingly cold.
